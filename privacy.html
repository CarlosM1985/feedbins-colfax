Privacy Policy

FeedBins (feedbins.app) — Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FeedBins ("we", "the app") collects, uses, and protects information from users of the web application available at https://feedbins.app and the FeedBins mobile app for Android.

1. Who we are

FeedBins is an operational management tool for farms: feed bin monitoring, level readings, feed orders, and supervision rounds. The data controller is the operator of FeedBins. Contact: soporte@feedbins.app.

2. Information we collect

FeedBins is an internal-use application with accounts created by an administrator. There is no public sign-up. We collect only:

  • Account data: email address and password (managed by Firebase Authentication; we never store passwords in plain text), the user's name, and their assigned role within the organization.
  • Operational data: bin level readings, feed orders, change history, and round logs, associated with the account that creates them.
  • Basic technical data: what Firebase requires to function, such as session identifiers.

The app does not collect: location, contacts, photos, microphone audio, advertising identifiers, or data from minors. We do not use behavioral analytics or advertising tools.

3. How we use information

  • To authenticate users and control access based on their role (administrator, manager, operator, or viewer).
  • To record and display farm operations: feed levels, orders, and rounds.
  • To maintain a change history for operational traceability.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Third-party services

FeedBins uses Google Firebase services (Google LLC) for authentication, database, and hosting. Data is stored on Google servers. You can review Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Cookies and local storage

The app uses cookies and local storage (localStorage) solely to keep you signed in and to save interface preferences. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

6. Data retention and deletion

Account data is retained while the account is active. Operational data is retained as part of the operation's historical record. To request deletion of your account and personal data, email soporte@feedbins.app and we will process your request within 30 days.

7. Security

Access to data is protected by authentication and security rules that limit what each user can view and modify based on their role. All communication takes place over encrypted connections (HTTPS).

8. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing soporte@feedbins.app. Depending on your state of residence (for example, under the California Consumer Privacy Act), you may have additional rights regarding your personal information; we will honor applicable requests.

9. Children's privacy

FeedBins is a workplace tool intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.

11. Contact

For any questions about this policy or how your data is handled: soporte@feedbins.app
